2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide to us:

    • 3. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information for:

  • Providing Services: Account creation, authentication, app functionality

  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

    • 4. Data Storage and Security

    4.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Data is stored on secure servers hosted by Abacus.AI
  • Database: PostgreSQL with encryption at rest
  • Location: United States (with global availability)

    • 4.2 Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • JWT tokens for secure authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates

    • 4.3 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup retention: Up to 90 days for disaster recovery
